How To Cook My Favourite Meal


Okro Soup


ingredients

  • Stockfish - pre soaked .
  • Dry fish - cleaned and soaked .
  • Ponmo(cow skin) - cooked .
  • Crayfish - 2 tablespoons, blended .
  • Pepper - scotch bonnet or chili (blended with onions) .
  • Seasoning cubes - like maggi or knorr .
  • Salt - to taste .
  • Ugwu (fluted pumpkin leaves ) or spinach - a handful, chopped .
  • Onions- 1 small (optional for extra flavour .
  • Water of meat stock .

Steps

  1. In a pot ,heat palm oil for about 30seconds (don't bleach it ) .
  2. Add blended pepper and a little onion ,fry for about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the proteins and stir well .
  4. Pour in some of the protein stock and let it simmer for like 2-3 minutes .
  5. Add crayfish, more seasoning cubes if needed, and salt to taste .
  6. Stir in chopped okro. You will notice the soup becomes stretchy/slimy .
  7. Finally , toss in the chopped ugwu or spinach leaves .
  8. Stir and let everything cook together for another 2-3 minutes .
  9. Check for salt and seasoning .

Summary

  It is easy to prepare with the right ingredients.
